Research Interests

I am interested in sensor fusion and state estimation, with applications in navigation and perception of autonomous systems. I am also interested in new sensors, like solid state lidar, infrared camera, 4D radar and event camera.

I am much interested in multi-motion estimation problem, which estimates objects and ego motion when the two body both keep moving. It relys on these following techniques:

  • Dynamic and Semantic SLAM

  • Object Detection, Tracking and Prediction

  • Multi Sensor Fusion State Estimation

  • Control,Signal Processing
